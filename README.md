# CLI App

### Rock-Paper-Scissors:

The RPS game has three actions you can select: 
1. Play Round 
Start a round by picking a choice between `rock`, `paper`, or `scissors`, then the computer will randomly select one of these options. Then, if your choice wins, you gain one point to your **wins** counter, and if you lose, you gain a point to your **losses** counter, and if you tie, your **tie** counter goes up by a point. 

2. View Stats
Show a **statistics** of how many games won, games lost, games tied, total games played, and the rate of games won.

3. Exit
 Will close the **App** while displaying the **result** of all your matches.


### Shopping Cart:

The Shopping Cart has four actions you can select: 
1. Add Item 
Enter an Item of your choosing into the **cart**, enter the item **quantity**, and the price **per** item. Your item and its details will be added to your cart.

2. Remove Item
Enter the Item you want to remove and the **amount** you want remove from your cart. 

3. View List
 Show your cart with **all** the items you have, their cost, the **total number** of items, and the **total price** of those items.

4. Exit 
You check out of the store and are told farewell by the store.

### Game Shows:

The Shopping Cart has three actions you can select: 
1. Start Quiz 
Start a **20-question** of **multiple choice** math quiz where every time you get a question right, you gain a point. At the end of the quiz, if you have a **high score**, you can add your name to a list of the **top five players**.  

2. View High Scores
View a **list** of players, showing their **name**, **scores**, and the **date** they played. 

3. Exit
Exit the game.
